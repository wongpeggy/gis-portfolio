About Me

I completed my Bachelor of Arts with Distinction in Psychology at University of Alberta in 2018. My first job as an insurance agent at Allstate has strengthened my communication and interpersonal skills with my colleagues and clients. After enjoying considerable success in the insurance industry, I became interested in GIS after taking an introductory course on Coursera in my spare time. As a GIS student at Fleming College, I have developed comprehensive knowledge in database development, workflow automation, and spatial analysis techniques. At the end of the program, I collaborated with two other students to develop a series of web maps and web applications for the City of Toronto to track all the active and inactive development activities in the city, which has allowed me to gain real world experience to leverage my practical skills and work ethic.
