Mount Fuji, Japan

View Map View Flowchart
CATALYST Professional ArcGIS Pro

The map displays satellite imagery of Mount Fuji, Japan, generated from Landsat 8 OLI imagery and SRTM elevation data from the United States Geological Survey (USGS) EarthExplorer. Various image enhancement strategies, such as pan-sharpening, high-pass differential filter, and shaded relief enhancement, were implemented to produce the final cartographic output for the Mount Fuji area.
