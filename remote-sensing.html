Mount Fuji, Japan

View Map View Flowchart
CATALYST Professional ArcGIS Pro

The map displays the satellite imagery of Mount Fuji, Japan, generated by the Landsat 8 OLI image and the SRTM Elevation data downloaded from USGS EarthExplorer. Various image enhancement strategies, such as pan-sharpening, high pass differential filter, and shaded relief enhancement, were implemented to produce a final cartographic output of the Mount Fuji area.
